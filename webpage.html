About Amazon

Amazon.com, Inc. is a multinational technology and e-commerce company based in Seattle, Washington. Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, Amazon started as an online bookstore and has grown into one of the largest online retailers in the world. The company operates across various sectors, including e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence.

Amazon is known for its diverse product offerings, ranging from electronics and books to groceries, clothing, and beyond. Its most popular products include the Amazon Echo, Kindle e-readers, and Fire TV. Additionally, the company has revolutionized logistics and supply chain management, with a vast network of fulfillment centers and advanced technologies.

Today, Amazon continues to innovate and expand globally, with services like Amazon Web Services (AWS), Amazon Prime, and Amazon Fresh. Whether it's shopping, streaming movies, or powering the cloud, Amazon is a central part of daily life for millions of people worldwide.
